Sticky Teriyaki Tofu

Description

This sticky teriyaki tofu is my favourite tofu recipe ever – so easy to make, but such an incredible flavour! This dish will convert any tofu hater.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 400 g (~ 14 oz) extra firm tofu, pressed and cut into bitesize pieces
  • 1 tsp minced garlic (I used garlic paste)
  • 1/2 tsp minced ginger (I used ginger paste)
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp mirin or white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp Chinese five spice

Steps

  1. Heat a dash of oil in a large frying pan, and add the pieces of tofu. Cook over a medium heat for around 10 minutes, turning the tofu over ever few minutes, until it’s crispy all over. You actually want to cook it until it’s a bit too dry, as it will rehydrate once the sauce is added.
  2. When the tofu is very dry and crispy, add the minced garlic and ginger. Cook for 1 more minute, then add the remaining ingredients. Bring to a simmer, and cook for a few more minutes until the sauce has become thick and sticky. If you want a slightly saucier dish, you can add a dash more water (or a little more of any of the sauce ingredients).
  3. Best served straight away.
